Neapolitan Pizza

We select the finest ingredients from our own favourites.

Our pizza is a hybrid yeast and sourdough resistant dough that has been fermented for over 48 hours.

The long proofing time together with cooking at an intense heat of 500°C results in a moist, light, soft, meltable crust that is aromatic and delicious.​
